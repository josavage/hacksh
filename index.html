Hack.sh

14-15 Mars 2014

Sherbrooke's first hackathon

Attend

Hack.sh March 14th 18:00 - March 15th 21:00

With us, you discovered Python, you discovered Ruby. Now is the time for you to come and apply what you've learned with other hackers, in a gathering never seen before in Sherbrooke.

With the help of Sherweb, Apius is giving you the opportunity to hack for 24 hours is the amazing new offices of the company. Sherweb will provide you with everything you need: beverages, food, candy, snacks, mentor, web hosting, lightning talks and more than 1000$ in prizes! Incredible but true, the event is totally free for the attendees!

Attend

Amazing prizes to win

Hack.sh main competition

The jury of Hack.sh will decide which three teams did best and they will each be awarded a prize.

Other competitions: Sherweb, Wolters Kluwer CCH, and PasswordBox

These three companies will each independently award a prize to the team they judged was best.

General rules

  • Maximum of four people per team
  • The project must be linked to computer science
  • The initial code base must be empty
  • Employees cannot win one of their employer's prizes
